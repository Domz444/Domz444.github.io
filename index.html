A liitle bit about us

Brick Lane Eatery is Century City's "Hidden Gem" tucked away along the Canals; where you can find fantastic food, reasonable prices, friendly staff, great service, dog friendly environment and free WiFi. Under new ownership, Brick Lane Eatery is geared to providing good quality, freshly made meals and excellent service with a team of friendly, attentive staff. The interior has an open-plan, industrial feel and it features a selection of tables in various shapes and sizes both inside and outside, ranging from small to large and comfortable bench seats with cushions.

Some additonal info

The outside seating area under the trees is a popular area for the families with kiddies who want to play in the sandpit; and in the evenings the courtyard lights up with fairy lights, with heaters and blankets when needed.

On the colder days our indoor fireplace keeps you warm and our wide selection of boardgames keeps you entertained. Brick Lane Eatery is also a Dog Friendly restaurant, and we even have special Cool Dogs Doggy Ice Cream on our menu!
